Cookie Policy

Last updated: 18 May 2026

This page explains the cookies and similar storage technologies used by PSPO·I Trainer, why they're used, and how you can control them. We rely on CookieYes to keep the list below accurate — it's regenerated automatically from a live scan of the site, so when we add or remove a tracker the table here updates within hours, without us needing to edit this page.

1. Cookies we use

Browser storage we use directly

Beyond what CookieYes lists above, the app keeps a handful of values in your browser's localStorage. These are first-party only, never transmitted, and exempt from prior consent under ePrivacy because they are strictly necessary to deliver the study tool you opened.

  • pspo-progress-v1 — your study progress (questions attempted, correct/incorrect counts, bookmarks). Use Reset progress under Stats in the app, or clear site data, to delete.
  • pspo-theme — remembers whether you last chose the Classic or Arcade theme so the app opens in the same skin next time.
  • pspo_mock_complete_unlocked — small flag preventing the "all required concepts mastered" achievement event from firing more than once.
  • pspo_flawless — remembers that you've earned the "Flawless Victory" trophy so it stays unlocked on the title screen.
  • pspo_flawless_event_fired — companion flag preventing the "Flawless Victory" analytics event from firing more than once per browser.

2. How to control cookies

You can change or withdraw your consent at any time via the cookie banner. To reopen it, click the small consent icon shown in the bottom-left of the page (placed there by CookieYes), or clear this site's cookies in your browser settings and reload — the banner will reappear.

You can also delete cookies and localStorage at any time via your browser's "Clear browsing data" or per-site settings. Deleting localStorage will erase your study progress.

3. Third-party links

Pages on this app may link to external sites (Scrum.org, scrumguides.org, Vercel, Google, CookieYes). Their cookie behaviour is governed by their own policies; we don't control them.

4. Changes

If we add or remove trackers, this page will be updated and the "Last updated" date refreshed. Material changes will reset the consent banner so you can review your choices.

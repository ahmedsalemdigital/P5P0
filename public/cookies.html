Cookie Policy

Last updated: 17 May 2026

This page explains the cookies and similar storage technologies used by PSPO·I Trainer, why they're used, and how you can control them.

1. What we use

Strictly necessary

  • cookieyes-consent — set by the CookieYes consent banner to remember your cookie preferences. Without it, you would be asked for consent on every page load. Provider: CookieYes. Duration: 1 year.
  • localStorage: pspo-progress-v1 — stores your study progress (questions attempted, correct/incorrect counts, bookmarks, theme preference). Stored only in your browser, not transmitted to us. Duration: until you clear browser data or use the in-app Reset progress control.
  • localStorage: pspo_mock_complete_unlocked — small flag that prevents the "all concepts mastered" achievement event from firing more than once.

Strictly-necessary storage is required to deliver a service you have explicitly requested (the study tool), and is therefore exempt from prior consent under ePrivacy.

Analytics — only with your consent

If you accept analytics in the consent banner, we load Google Analytics 4 via Google Tag Manager. GA4 sets the following:

  • _ga, _ga_<container-id> — Google Analytics client identifiers used to distinguish anonymous visitors and sessions. Provider: Google LLC. Duration: up to 2 years.

IP addresses are anonymised by Google before storage. We do not enable advertising features or Google Signals. No data is shared with advertisers.

Maintainer note: for the most accurate and up-to-date cookie list, log into your CookieYes dashboard, run a fresh scan of the production domain, and paste the auto-generated table in place of the lists above. CookieYes also offers an auto-updating embed if you prefer.

2. How to control cookies

You can change or withdraw your consent at any time via the cookie banner. To reopen it, click the small consent icon shown in the bottom-left of the page (placed there by CookieYes), or clear this site's cookies in your browser settings and reload — the banner will reappear.

You can also delete cookies and localStorage at any time via your browser's "Clear browsing data" or per-site settings. Deleting localStorage will erase your study progress.

3. Third-party links

Pages on this app may link to external sites (Scrum.org, scrumguides.org, Vercel, Google, CookieYes). Their cookie behaviour is governed by their own policies; we don't control them.

4. Changes

If we add or remove trackers, this page will be updated and the "Last updated" date refreshed. Material changes will reset the consent banner so you can review your choices.
